Bali

Bali is an island and province of Indonesia, situated at the western end of the Lesser Sunda Islands archipelago, between the islands of Java (to the west) and Lomboque (to the east).

Paris

Since the seventeenth century, Paris has been one of Europe's leading centers of finance, diplomacy, commerce, fashion, science, and the arts. The city of Paris is the center and seat of government of the administrative region of Île-de-France, which has an estimated population in 2020 of 12,278,210 inhabitants, or about 18% of France's population.

Roma

It is the capital of Italy and also of the Lazio region, as well as being the center of the Metropolitan City of Rome and a special commune. With 2,860,009 residents in 1,285 km², Rome is the most populous commune in the country and the third most populous city in the European Union.

Veneza

Venice is a city in northeastern Italy situated on a group of 117 small islands separated by canals and connected by bridges.It is located in the swampy Venetian Lagoon, which stretches along the coast between the mouths of the Po and Piave rivers. Venice is famous for the beauty of its architecture and artwork. A part of the city is listed as a World Heritage Site, along with its lagoon.
